1. About TechFest2K26

A 24-hour product-focused hackathon where you build end-to-end solutions – from idea to deployment – mentored by industry experts and alumni working at top tech companies.

What to Expect

Brainstorm with your squad, ship a working MVP, and pitch to a jury of engineers, founders, and faculty. Get real-time feedback, improve your GitHub repo, and build something that survives beyond the hackathon.

• 24-Hour Non-Stop Coding
• Mentor Office Hours
• Night-long Snacks
• Networking & Swags

Who Should Apply

If you love shipping projects, exploring new stacks, or just want to experience a real startup-style sprint, this hackathon is for you. All branches and years are welcome.

• Beginner-Friendly Tracks
• Advanced Challenges
• Cross-Branch Teams
• On-Spot Surprises
